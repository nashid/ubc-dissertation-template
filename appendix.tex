\chapter{Supporting Materials}

Replace or remove this appendix as needed.  Appendices hold
supporting material not central to the dissertation, for example:
\begin{itemize}
\item additional details of methodology and/or data;
\item diagrams of specialized equipment developed;
\item copies of questionnaires and survey instruments;
\item a list of other publications completed during the degree but
not included as chapters.
\end{itemize}

Copies of any required UBC Research Ethics Board Certificates of
Approval must also be included as an appendix (the
\latexpackage{pdfpages} package is useful for this).
